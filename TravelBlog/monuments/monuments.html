Monuments

Charminar, Hyderabad

The Charminar, an iconic symbol of Hyderabad built in 1591 by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah, showcases stunning Indo-Islamic architecture with intricate stucco designs. It offers panoramic views of the bustling Laad Bazaar and surrounding cityscape, making it a must-visit landmark.Renowned for its Irani chai and Hyderabadi biryani.

Golconda Fort, Hyderabad

A majestic fortress known for its acoustics and advanced water system. Famous for being a diamond market where the Koh-i-Noor diamond was once stored. Offers spectacular city views from the top of the fort.
