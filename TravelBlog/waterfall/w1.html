Kuntala Waterfalls, Telangana

Nestled in the Adilabad district of Telangana, Kuntala Waterfalls is the highest waterfall in the state, cascading from a height of 147 feet. Surrounded by dense forests and serene landscapes, it’s a perfect getaway for nature lovers, photographers, and adventure seekers.

Highlights:

  • Highest waterfall in Telangana
  • Lush greenery and scenic beauty
  • Ideal for picnics and photography
  • Monsoon magic with enhanced water flow

Quick Info:

  • Location: Adilabad District, Telangana
  • Height: 147 feet
  • Best Time to Visit: July–September (Monsoon Season)
  • Activities: Nature walks, trekking, photography
