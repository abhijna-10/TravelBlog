Kuntala WaterFalls, Telangana

it is the highest waterfall in Telangana, offering a spectacular sight with cascading water flowing through rocks, creating a scenic and untouched beauty, especially during monsoon season when the water flow is at its peak; you can also enjoy trekking around the boulders to reach the base of the falls and take in the surrounding natural landscape.

Papavinasam Theertham, AndhraPradesh

People visit Papavinasam Theertham, located near Tirupati, primarily for its religious significance, as it is believed that bathing in its holy water washes away one's sins, with "Papa" meaning sin and "Vinasam" meaning destroyer; essentially, it's considered a place to cleanse oneself spiritually by taking a dip in the sacred water.

Jog Falls, Karnataka

Jog Falls is a spectacular waterfall in Karnataka, India that is known for its height, beauty, and setting.The best time to visit Jog Falls in Karnataka is during the monsoon season, from June to September. During this time, the Sharavathi River is at its fullest, and the falls are at their most powerful.
